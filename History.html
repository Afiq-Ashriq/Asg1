HISTORY

Formation of Rammstein (1994)

Rammstein was founded in Berlin, 1994. The founding fathers of the band are Richard Kruspe and Christoph Schneider. Richard Kruspe invited Till Lindemann to Rammstein after hearing him sing while he was working. In 1994, there was a contest for amateur bands and the winner would receive the opportunity to record a four-track demo audiotape in a proffesional studio.

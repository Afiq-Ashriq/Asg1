HISTORY

Formation of Rammstein (1994)

Rammstein was founded in Berlin, 1994. The founding fathers of the band are Richard Kruspe and Christoph Schneider. Richard was living with Oliver Riedel of The Inchtabokatables and Christoph "Doom" Schneider at the time (of Die Firma). Richard realized that the music he had previously created did not suit him; he envisioned a sound that combined machines and the sound of heavy guitars. The three began collaborating on a new project. Richard soon discovered that writing both music and lyrics at the same time was difficult and invited Till Lindemann to Rammstein after hearing him sing while he was working. In 1994, there was a contest for amateur bands and the winner would receive the opportunity to record a four-track demo audiotape in a proffesional studio.

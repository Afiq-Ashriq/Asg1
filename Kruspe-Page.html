Richard Kruspe

Full Name: Sven Kruspe
Date of birth: 24 June 1967
Height: 1.80m (5'11")

Biograpghy

Richard Kruspe was born in Wittenberge, German Democratic Republic. His birth name is actually Zven Kruspe but changed his name to Richard at the age of 16. He is the lead guitarist and background singer for Rammstein. Kruspe had a tough childhood, his parents divorced and his mother remarried. His did not get along with his stepfather and he would run away from home. To deal with anger he joined wrestling in his school.

Fun facts

  • He used to wrestle before Rammstein
  • He wrote the music for his own wedding

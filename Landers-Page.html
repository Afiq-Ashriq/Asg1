Paul Landers

Birth Name: Heiko Paul Hiersche
Date of birth: 9 December 1964
Height: 1.71m (5'7")

Biograpghy

Paul Landers was born in Baumschulenweg on December 9, 1964. He is the rhythm guitarist for the band. He lived in Moscow, Russian for a bit when he was young. He can speak russian but cannot read and write russian. Paul Landers married to Nikki Landers in 1984 and he actually to his wife's surname which is why his name is Paul Landers. He kept his name after his divorce with Nikki. Landers and Christian 'Flake' Lorenz were band mates for the band "feeling B" before Rammstein and they were living with each other.

Fun facts

  • He is the shortest rammstein member
  • He does not seem to like rock but enjoys it with his band Rammstein

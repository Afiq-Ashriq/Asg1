Till Lindemann

Full Name: Till Lindemann
Date of birth: 4 January 1963
Height: 1.85m (6'1)

Biograpghy

Till lindemann is the lead singer for Rammstein. He is also known as the leader of Rammstein. He was born in Leipzig, East Germany. Before Rammstein, Lindemann was a drummer for a band called First Arsch. Lindemann has appeared in the "50 Greatest Metal Frontmen of All Time" by RoadRunner Records.

Fun fact

  • Lindemann is a qualified pyrotechnician,actor and poet

Christian Lorenz

Full Name: Christian Lorenz
Date of birth: 16 November 1966
Height: 1.98m (6'5")

Biograpghy

Chrstian 'Flake' Lorenz was born in East Berlin on November 16, 1966. He is the keyboardist for the band. He is also one of the main composers besides Richard Kruspe. Lorenz is a well educated pianist. He wanted to learn the piano because his childhood friend played the piano when they were 3 years old. His parents sent him to music school. He used a marker to make a makeshift piano on the window ledge and on his 15th birthday his parents bought for him his first piano.

He got his nickname from a TV show "Vicky the Viking" where the main character lives in a village named Flake.

Fun fact

  • He did not want to join Rammstein at first because he wanted to become a doctor.

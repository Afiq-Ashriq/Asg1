Biograpghy

Christoph "Doom" Schneider was born in Pankow, East Berlin, German Democratic Republic on May 11, 1966. He is the drummer for Rammstein. He was a gymnast and stopped when he was 16 and went on to work as a telecommunications assistant. He served his national service with the East German Army in 1984 which makes him the only Rammstein member to have served in the army. Schneider's parents were classical music lovers and had Schneider sent to a special school to learn the trumpet, clarinet or trombone. Schneider chose to learn the trumpet and he was talented. However, Scheider really wanted to play the drums. His parents were not supportive of him wanting to play the drums so Schneider practiced with sticks and built his drum set with only tin cans and buckets.

Schneider got his nickname Doom from his favourite video game named Doom.
